The Zone of Alignment Audit

Choosing a niche is the number one hurdle for new coaches and entrepreneurs. Most people either choose a niche based purely on what they are passionate about (a hobby) or purely on what makes money (a grind).

The Zone of Alignment Audit helps clients find the precise intersection where a sustainable, joyful business lives.

Circle 1: Deep Competence

What are you effortlessly good at? What do people naturally ask you for advice on? This is your baseline skillset. (Note: Do not confuse this with a degree or certification. It is innate capability).

Circle 2: Genuine Curiosity

What could you read 10 books about and not get bored? What do you research for fun on a Saturday morning? (If a client has Competence but lacks Curiosity, they will eventually burn out because the work feels like a chore).

Circle 3: Definite Market

What are people currently spending money to solve right now? Is there a bleeding neck problem? (If a client has Competence and Curiosity but lacks Market, they do not have a business; they have an expensive hobby).

Execution: The Sweet Spot

Draw a Venn diagram of these three circles. Ask the client to brain-dump 10 ideas into each circle. Discard anything that doesn’t fit in the exact center overlap. That center is their Zone of Alignment.
